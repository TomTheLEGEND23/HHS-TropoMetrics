# Network Design
## Cabling
LAB (External Interface) - ISP(GE0/0/1)
ISP(Se0/1/0) - Delft-R1(Se0/1/0)
ISP(Se0/1/1) - Delft-R2(Se0/1/1)
ISP(GE0/0/0) - Aruba-R1(GE0/0/0)
Aruba-R1(GE0/0/1) - Aruba-SW1(GE0/0/0)
Aruba-SW1(Fa0/1) - PC3(VLan210)(FA0/1)
Delft-R1(GE0/0/0) - Delft-MLS1(GE1/0/1)

Delft-R2(GE0/0/1) - Delft-MLS2(GE1/0/1)
Delft-R2(GE0/0/0) - Delft-MLS1(GE1/0/2)
Delft-MLS1(GE1/0/3) - Delft-MLS2(GE1/0/3) #Portchannel 1
Delft-MLS1(GE1/0/4) - Delft-MLS2(GE1/0/4) #PortChannel 1
Delft-MLS1(GE1/0/5) - Delft-SW1(GE0/1)
Delft-MLS2(GE1/0/5) - Delft-SW2(GE0/1)
Delft-MLS1(GE1/0/6) - Delft-SW2(GE0/2)
Delft-MLS2(GE1/0/6) - Delft-SW1(GE0/2)
Delft-SW1(FA0/1) - PC1(Vlan10)(FA0/1)
Delft-SW1(FA0/2) - AP(Vlan80)(FA0/1)
Delft-SW3(FA0/1) - PC2(Vlan20)(FA0/1)
Delft-MLS2(GE1/0/7) - Delft-SW3(GE0/1) #Portchannel 2
Delft-MLS2(GE1/0/8) - Delft-SW3(GE0/2) #Portchannel 2
Delft-SW3(FA0/1) - Service(Fast Port)
